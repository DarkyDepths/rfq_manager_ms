Reminder rules (email)
How the system reminds teams and managers before deadlines.
  • Rule 1: For each RFQ stage with a due date, send an email reminder to the assigned team 5 days before due date.
  • Rule 2: If the stage progress < 80% two days before due date, send a reminder to team + RFQ owner.
  • Rule 3: If the due date is exceeded and stage still open, send daily digest to RFQ owner and Management until closed.
  • Rule 4: For critical RFQs (deadline < 7 days), highlight tasks in red in the Dashboard and Workspace.
